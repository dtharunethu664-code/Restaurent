About Savoria

Savoria is a premium dining restaurant located in Sri Lanka, offering a rich fusion of Sri Lankan, Indian, and international cuisines. Founded in 2020 by Mrs. Dahara Tharunethu, Savoria was created with a passion for authentic flavors, quality ingredients, and warm hospitality.

From traditional Sri Lankan favorites to modern global dishes, every plate at Savoria is crafted with care to deliver a memorable dining experience.
